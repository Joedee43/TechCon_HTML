Our History

Since its inception in 2010, TechCon has grown from a small regional meetup to a world-class technology conference drawing innovators and industry giants from around the globe.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to inspire, educate, and empower the tech community by bringing together the brightest minds to share knowledge, collaborate on solutions, and push the boundaries of what's possible.
